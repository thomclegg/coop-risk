Ability - Insight #6

We’re making risk inaccessible to people

We believe...

We're making something that people are already put off about more unappealing and inaccessible because we write for ourselves, not our audience.

Because we've seen and heard...

"Risk is a turn off, it needs to be in plain English"

"You need a degree in risk to understand it"

"We have a regular session called ‘What's on your mind’ where we discuss risks affecting the stores...If we called it a risk meeting no one would come!"

“If you make it overly complicated, people lose interest”

“I would never give a member of my team the framework to read - who is it for?”

“The difficulty with risk is explaining it to the people on the ground - it needs to be simple”

Currently its one delivery to all - it “should be tailored to the audience.”

46% of respondents were disatisfied or unaware of risk guidence on the intranet
