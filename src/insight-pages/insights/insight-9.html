Motivation - Insight #9

Getting stuff done can be an uphill struggle

We believe...

For some risk colleagues it’s a battle to get stuff done because business areas don't always provide the time, resources, support and skills needed to do it properly.

Because we've seen and heard...

"I challenge risk inputters to update the register Bi-Monthly. If they didn't it wouldn't get done."

“I have a risk category owner who doesn't take ownership”

In some areas, dedicated individuals trying to manage risk to the best of their ability

They often are plugging away in areas who don’t value risk management and so they face an uphill battle

They’re hassling people for updates and information time and again

They’re filling in risk registers for people
