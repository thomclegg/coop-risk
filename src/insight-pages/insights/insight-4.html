Motivation - Insight #4

Hard to see benefits of the risk register

We believe...

People don't see the benefit of filling in the risk register, they see it getting in the way of them managing their risks because it’s hard to use and doesn’t add value.

Because we've seen and heard...

"I fill in the risk register because the risk team ask me to...I don’t know what they do with it"

"I know risks are being managed so I don't prioritise the risk reg as it takes so long"

"I last looked at the Risk Register 3 months ago - I assume it has not changed"

I do it to keep the risk team happy

I know risks are being managed so I don't prioritise the risk reg as it takes so long

I aim to be thorough filling in the risk reg but it is so time consuming

We are managing risk but I have to put it into a form the risk team understand Risk management is currently spreadsheet wrangling rather than having meaningful conversations

98% of respondents understnd the reason they fill in the risk register.

37% of respondents didnt feel the risk register helps them manage risk.
