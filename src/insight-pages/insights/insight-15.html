Ability - Insight #15

People don’t always know what good risk management is

We believe...

There is a discrepancy between how well people think they’re managing risk and how well the business partner thinks they’re managing risk because people don’t know what good risk management is.

Because we've seen and heard...

“We need to highlight where risk is being managed poorly”

BPs mention areas where teams aren’t doing as well as hoped

"I think they’re struggling, it’ll be interesting to see how they rate themselves”

Lack of understanding of risk:
- “We have not hit our targets” is not a risk, it's happened, its now an issue.
- Writing down every risk is not possible - you need to prioritise the key ones
- Risk does not need to be Blue Sky thinking - if it's not likely to happen in 100 years, I don't want to see it

User need #1

As a risk expert
I need to be able to help people manage risk in the right way.
So they can improve their chances of success.

User need #2

As a Co-op manager
I need to know what may negatively impact my area
So that I can take steps to reduce the impact

User need #3

As a Co-op manager
I need to feel confident that my team are managing our risks
So that I can get on with delivering, and not feel I need to worry about risk

User need #4

As a Co-op manager
I need to be able to see all risks that could impact my areas
So we can take action to reduce the impact and likelihood

User need #5

As a risk business partner
I need to be able to see areas I’m responsible for are managing risk well
So I can step in when needed and provide assurance to senior managers

User need #13

When I’m managing risk
I need to know that I’m doing the right thing
So that I don’t feel worried or concerned

User need #14

When I’m managing risk
I need to feel I’m doing a good job
So that I feel a sense of purpose and reward

User need #15

When I’m managing my risks
I need to know what to do and how to do it
So that I can manage the risks in the most appropriate way

User need #18

As a risk colleague

User need #20

When I’m working on risk

So that they can understand and act on the information.
