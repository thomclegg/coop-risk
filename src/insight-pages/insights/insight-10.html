Motivation - Insight #10

Getting stuff done can be an uphill struggle

We believe...

A lack of clear roles and responsibilities makes it easy for people to avoid doing what they need to… And harder for people on the ground to get others to take action.

Because we've seen and heard...

"Risk owners should have more oversight of what their team are doing"

“People don't understand the impact of what they are doing” - accepting a risk without mitigation

Risk owners do not embrace their role. Not pro-active!

There needs to be more training for people involved in risk. So they understand the expectation

They’re hassling people for updates and information time and again

Getting some risk managers to prioritise risk is the biggest challenge

25% of respondees with risk in their role dont have risk in their goal

58% of respondees have risk management in your job description
