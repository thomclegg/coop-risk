Ability - Insight #20

Conversation is a useful tool, but it’s time consuming

We believe...

Conversation is used a lot in managing risk. Often, it’s the best way to work through an issue, such as analysis.
At other times it’s being used to solve shortcomings, such as ‘linking’ risks and explaining risk in ‘human’ language

Because we've seen and heard...

"I take real life scenarios and apply a risk filter to it in order to explain what Risk is. Normally people get it that way."

Lots of meetings to gather and share updates.

A lot of time is spent explaining the Risk Framework and Register.

"I only find out about risks by going out there and turning over a few stones."

When asked about how they demonstrate they manage risk, respondees said:
- 39% Talk about it
- 22% Use the risk register
- 17% Use a risk summary report
- 20% Other
