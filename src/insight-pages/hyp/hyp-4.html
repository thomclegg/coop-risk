Hypotheses #4

We believe if we work with our users to gather feedback, test and iterate our processes, tools and guidance

We'll see people using the standard tools and processes because it helps them manage risk effectively and efficiently already, they don’t need to change their own. People will feel empowered and valued

We’ll know this is true when we see a significant reduction in the number of user created documents and tools, and deviations from the standard process

Considerations

  • All users, BPs, to side of desk inputters, don’t just speak to the ‘yes people’ or the same people, get a range. Consider that some people have disabilities that may impact how they use or interpret technology and information
  • You need to act on what is said and feed it back to people, otherwise they will feel ignored
  • Ensure you’re understanding what people need, not what they say they want
  • Select suppliers or partners who will buy into this way of working
  • BPs spend half a day every six weeks with an end user to understand how they work
