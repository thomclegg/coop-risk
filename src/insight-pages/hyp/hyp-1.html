What we did Working principles

Hypotheses #1

We believe that if we build risk management into the objectives and job descriptions of all relevant people

We'll see risk taken more seriously by people, with the appropriate actions and effort taken and risk people on the ground able to manage and coordinate risks in their teams more easily. People will feel recognised for the work they do

We’ll know this is true when we see risk actions taken more proactively on risk registers, positive feedback from co-ordinators both ad hoc and as part of a quarterly survey, and an increase in the number of risk objectives recorded in MyHR

Considerations

  • This should apply to all people who deal with risk, including ‘side of deskers’
  • It needs HR support and buy it
  • It requires the roles to be identified by Group Risk in conjunction with your users
  • It needs to be communicated clearly, using the language your users use
  • It needs to involve the user in the design to identify a consistent role set and description
  • It needs to be supported by you
