Hypotheses #6

We believe that if we provide people with a way to access and understand all the relevant risk information they need.

We'll see people better able to spot patterns and issues that impact them

We’ll know this is true when the time it takes to provide/share information for others (such senior managers, the exec etc) is reduced by x, we receive positive feedback from co-ordinators from user testing, ad hoc comments and as part of a quarterly survey

#5

Considerations

  • Think about outcomes not solutions, people don’t want a report, they want information and assurance. The report is one possible solution
  • More work is needed to identify what information people need access to
  • Consider the impact on trust and relationships if people’s work is more visible, encourage openness, don’t blame
  • Consider how information is presented so that people can understand it
  • It needs to be communicated clearly, using the language your users use
  • It should be iterated, tested and improved
  • Ensure accessibility is considered
