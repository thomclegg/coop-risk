What we did Working principles

Business partner observation #10

We believe...

When Risk colleagues are looking at a risk rating they need to be able to see what it relates to.

Because we've seen and heard...

Ambiguity around financial impact (Profit, Income, Etc).

Risk ratings don't actually match up and relate to financial impact.

Risk dependency across departments - A risk has controls in another department.

5x5 does not translate well for category owners. It groups Financial and Reputation.

Risk is probability and pounds, that's relatable - unlike the 5x5.

5x5 does not forecast - where will we be in 12 months?
