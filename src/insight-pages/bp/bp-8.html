Business partner observation #8

We believe...

Most business areas believe they manage their own risks adequately.

Because we've seen and heard...

"It's all about recording the risk, not doing anything about it."

Finance manage risk 'instinctively' but they don't use a risk register.

Teams think they are effective but have no way to show it.

They might be doing it 95% right but its not visible to others or challengeable.

Investment in identifying issues is wasted if a risk does not get mitigated.

Documenting risk has contributed to a culture of considering risk.

Risk framework is not driving the exec to spend money in the right areas.

Keeping the risk register up to date requires more people or dedicated time..
