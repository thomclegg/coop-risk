Motivation - Insight #3

Risk is dismissed as ‘part of the job'

We believe...

Applying and following a risk process is not yet embedded in Co-op because people don’t see the benefit of doing it

Because we've seen and heard...

“People manage risk, day in day out- however they don't get the time or see the need to update the risk register”

“Behaviours won't change unless people can see there is something for them in it - What is the benefit to me / Co-op Group”

People know how to do their jobs well, and they generally know to take certain measures whilst doing it

Filling in the spreadsheet take significant effort, and people don’t see the point

People dismiss it as admin work

They’re turned off and daunted by the idea of a risk process
